Pesto Salmon with Roasted Vegetable Medley

Per serving: ~540 cal, 46g protein, 14g carbs, 32g fat. Week 4 rotation.

Servings: 5 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 35 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 lbs salmon, cut into 5 portions
  • 1 cup basil pesto (check for no added sugar/grains)
  • 2 lbs Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 1.5 lbs bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 lb cherry tomatoes
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • Lemon wedges

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425F.
  2. Toss Brussels sprouts, peppers, tomatoes with olive oil and seasoning. Spread on sheet pans, roast 20 minutes.
  3. Top each salmon portion with pesto. Add to pans, roast 10-12 minutes until salmon flakes.
  4. Cool, portion vegetables and salmon into 5 containers, sprinkle pine nuts on top.

Notes: Pesto helps salmon stay moist on reheat.

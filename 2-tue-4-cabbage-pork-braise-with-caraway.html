2 Tue 4: Cabbage & Pork Braise with Caraway

Per serving: ~520 cal, 44g protein, 16g carbs, 30g fat. Tuesday — Liver Support theme. Cabbage + sulfur vegetables for phase II detox.

Servings: 4 Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 40 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs pork shoulder, cut into 1.5-inch chunks
  • 2 lbs green cabbage, sliced into wedges
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp caraway seeds
  • 2 tbsp whole grain mustard
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Brown pork chunks in olive oil, 6 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Add onion, caraway, and mustard. Stir 2 minutes.
  3. Add cabbage wedges and broth. Cover, simmer 35-40 minutes until pork and cabbage are tender.
  4. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Freezes exceptionally well - flavors deepen after freeze/thaw cycle.

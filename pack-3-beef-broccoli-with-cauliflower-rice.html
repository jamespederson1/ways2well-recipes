Pack 3: Beef & Broccoli with Cauliflower Rice

Per serving: ~520 cal, 50g protein, 16g carbs, 28g fat. Week 4 rotation.

Servings: 5 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 20 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 lbs flank steak or sirloin, sliced thin
  • 3 tbsp avocado oil
  • 2 lbs broccoli florets
  • 2 lbs riced cauliflower
  • 1/3 cup coconut aminos
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp ginger
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Directions

  1. Toss beef with 2 tbsp coconut aminos. Sear in hot pan with avocado oil, 2 minutes per batch. Set aside.
  2. Steam or saute broccoli until bright green and tender-crisp, 5 minutes.
  3. Saute cauliflower rice in same pan with salt, 6 minutes.
  4. Whisk sauce ingredients (coconut aminos, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes), combine with beef and broccoli, simmer 2 minutes.
  5. Portion cauliflower rice into 5 containers, top with beef and broccoli.

Notes: Freezes very well.

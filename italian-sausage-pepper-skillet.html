Italian Sausage & Pepper Skillet

Per serving: ~540 cal, 44g protein, 17g carbs, 32g fat. Week 1 rotation lunch.

Servings: 5 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 25 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 lbs Italian chicken or turkey sausage (sugar-free), sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 bell peppers (mixed colors), sliced
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 1 lb mushrooms, quartered
  • 1 lb spinach
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Brown sausage slices 5-6 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, saute onions and peppers 6 minutes until softened.
  3. Add mushrooms and garlic, cook 4 more minutes.
  4. Stir in spinach in batches until wilted. Return sausage to pan with seasonings. Cook 2 minutes to combine.
  5. Cool, portion into 5 containers, top each with parmesan.

Notes: Check sausage label for added sugar. Bilinski's, Applegate, and Aidells are sugar-free options.

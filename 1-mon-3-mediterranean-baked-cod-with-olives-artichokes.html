1 Mon 3: Mediterranean Baked Cod with Olives & Artichokes

Per serving: ~490 cal, 48g protein, 16g carbs, 26g fat. Monday — Lipid & Cardio theme. Lean white fish with heart-healthy Mediterranean fats.

Servings: 4 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 20 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs cod fillets (thick center-cut)
  • 1 jar (14 oz) artichoke hearts in olive oil, drained and halved
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 lb cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in skillet. Saute mushrooms and garlic 4 minutes.
  2. Add drained tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer 3 minutes. Transfer to baking dish.
  3. Scatter artichokes and olives over sauce. Place cod on top. Drizzle remaining oil.
  4. Bake 15-18 minutes until cod flakes. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Use thick center-cut cod for best freezer texture.

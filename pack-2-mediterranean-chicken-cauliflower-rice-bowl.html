Pack 2: Mediterranean Chicken & Cauliflower Rice Bowl

Per serving: ~510 cal, 52g protein, 18g carbs, 26g fat. Week 1 rotation lunch.

Servings: 5 Prep: 25 minutes Cook: 30 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp olive oil (plus 2 tbsp for cooking)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 lbs riced cauliflower (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 large cucumber, diced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 8 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped

Directions

  1. Marinate chicken in olive oil, lemon, garlic, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Cook chicken 6-7 minutes per side until internal temp hits 165F. Rest 5 minutes, then dice.
  3. In the same pan, saute riced cauliflower 5-7 minutes with salt and a splash of olive oil until tender but not mushy. Let cool.
  4. Divide cauliflower rice across 5 freezer-safe containers (about 1.5 cups each). Top each with 8 oz cooked chicken.
  5. Portion cucumber, tomatoes, olives, feta, and parsley into 5 separate small fridge containers - these do NOT freeze. Add to the bowl the day you eat it.
  6. Cool completely before sealing. Freeze the chicken/cauliflower portions; refrigerate the topping kits.

Notes: Freezer note: feta and cucumber don't freeze. Pack separately in fridge and assemble day-of.

Categories: Lunch, Grain-Free, Week 1

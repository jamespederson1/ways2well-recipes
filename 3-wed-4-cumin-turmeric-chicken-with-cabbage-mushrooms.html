3 Wed 4: Cumin-Turmeric Chicken with Cabbage & Mushrooms

Per serving: ~520 cal, 46g protein, 14g carbs, 30g fat. Wednesday — Metabolic/Insulin theme. Turmeric + cumin + cinnamon spice trio for glucose.

Servings: 4 Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 25 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1.5 lbs green cabbage, thick-sliced
  • 1 lb cremini mushrooms, quartered
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp ground cumin
  • 2 tbsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in Dutch oven. Brown chicken 6 minutes.
  2. Add onion and garlic, cook 4 minutes. Stir in cumin, turmeric, and cinnamon, 30 seconds.
  3. Add mushrooms, cook 3 minutes.
  4. Add cabbage and broth. Cover, simmer 15 minutes until cabbage wilts and chicken is cooked through.
  5. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Add black pepper to enhance turmeric absorption.

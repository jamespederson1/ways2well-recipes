5 Fri 2: Buffalo Chicken Cauliflower Casserole

Per serving: ~520 cal, 48g protein, 12g carbs, 30g fat. Friday — Anti-Inflammatory theme. Capsaicin from hot sauce supports inflammation markers.

Servings: 4 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 45 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1.5 lbs cauliflower florets, cut small
  • 1/2 cup Frank's RedHot (check no added sugar)
  • 4 tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup ranch seasoning (dry blend)
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Poach or bake chicken 20 minutes, shred.
  2. Mix melted butter, hot sauce, cream cheese, and ranch seasoning in large bowl.
  3. Toss shredded chicken and cauliflower florets in sauce mixture. Transfer to 9x13 baking dish.
  4. Top with cheddar. Bake 25 minutes.
  5. Cool completely, portion into 4 containers, freeze.

Notes: No green onion garnish — this is freezer-to-plate.

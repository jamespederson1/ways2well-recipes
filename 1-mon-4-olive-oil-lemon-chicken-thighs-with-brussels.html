1 Mon 4: Olive-Oil Lemon Chicken Thighs with Brussels

Per serving: ~530 cal, 48g protein, 14g carbs, 30g fat. Monday — Lipid & Cardio theme. Heavy EVOO for LDL support.

Servings: 4 Prep: 15 minutes (+20 min marinate, optional) Cook: 28 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice (about 3 lemons)
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1.5 lbs Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Whisk 1/4 cup olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Coat chicken thighs. Marinate 20 minutes (optional).
  2. Preheat oven to 425F. Toss Brussels sprouts with remaining olive oil and salt. Spread on sheet pan.
  3. Arrange chicken on top of Brussels. Scatter olives around.
  4. Roast 25-28 minutes until chicken registers 165F. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Thighs stay moist after freezer better than breasts.

Cajun Shrimp & Andouille Skillet

Per serving: ~520 cal, 48g protein, 13g carbs, 30g fat. Week 3 rotation.

Servings: 5 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 20 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 lb andouille sausage (sugar-free), sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 lb okra, sliced (or green beans)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning (no sugar)
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • Hot sauce, fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Heat oil in large skillet. Brown andouille 4 minutes, remove.
  2. Saute peppers, onion, okra 6 minutes. Add garlic and Cajun seasoning, cook 1 minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and simmer 5 minutes. Return sausage to pan.
  4. Add shrimp last, cook just 3-4 minutes until pink. Don't overcook.
  5. Cool, portion into 5 containers.

Notes: Shrimp can get rubbery on reheat - undercook slightly during prep.

3 Wed 1: Thai Coconut Curry Beef with Vegetables

Per serving: ~540 cal, 44g protein, 16g carbs, 32g fat. Wednesday — Metabolic/Insulin theme. Curry spices support glucose control.

Servings: 4 Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 15 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs beef sirloin, thin-sliced
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp red curry paste (check label, no added sugar)
  • 1.5 lbs mixed stir-fry vegetables (broccoli, bell pepper, snow peas)
  • 2 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp avocado oil
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Heat avocado oil in large skillet over high heat. Sear beef in batches, 90 seconds per batch. Remove.
  2. Add curry paste, ginger, garlic, and turmeric to pan, stir 30 seconds.
  3. Pour in coconut milk. Simmer 3 minutes.
  4. Add vegetables. Cook 5-6 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Return beef and add fish sauce. Simmer 2 minutes.
  6. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Coconut milk sauce keeps beef tender through freeze cycle.

1 Mon 2: Pesto Salmon with Green Beans & Bell Peppers

Per serving: ~540 cal, 46g protein, 12g carbs, 34g fat. Monday — Lipid & Cardio theme.

Servings: 4 Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 20 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs salmon fillets
  • 1/2 cup basil pesto (verify olive oil only, no seed oils)
  • 1.5 lbs green beans, trimmed
  • 1 lb red and yellow bell peppers, cut into strips
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Toss green beans and peppers with 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread vegetables on sheet pan. Place salmon on top and spread 2 tbsp pesto per fillet.
  3. Bake 18 minutes. Cool completely, portion into 4 containers.
  4. To reheat: Thaw overnight in fridge. Oven or microwave with damp paper towel.

Notes: Pesto helps salmon stay moist on reheat.

Ways2Well Recipes

How to import into AnyList:
  1. Open each recipe link below in a new tab.
  2. Click the AnyList extension icon in the Edge toolbar.
  3. The extension reads the schema.org Recipe data and saves it to your AnyList account.
  4. Repeat for each of the 14 recipes.

Or, use py launch_edge.py to open all 14 tabs at once.

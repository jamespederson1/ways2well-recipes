Pack 2: Korean-Style Beef Lettuce Cups (Bulgogi)

Per serving: ~520 cal, 50g protein, 14g carbs, 28g fat. Week 2 rotation.

Servings: 5 Prep: 25 minutes Cook: 15 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 lbs flank steak, sliced thin against the grain
  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp avocado oil (for cooking)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp gochujang (check for no grain)
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 2 lbs napa cabbage, shredded
  • 1 lb shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 bunch scallions, sliced
  • 2 heads butter lettuce (store separately, fresh)

Directions

  1. Marinate beef in coconut aminos, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, vinegar, and gochujang for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat avocado oil in a large skillet over high heat. Sear beef in batches, 2 minutes per batch. Set aside.
  3. In same pan, cook mushrooms 4 minutes, then cabbage 5 minutes until wilted.
  4. Combine beef, vegetables, and scallions. Cool, portion into 5 containers.
  5. Pack butter lettuce separately in fridge, wrap in damp paper towels. Spoon beef mixture into lettuce cups at lunch.

Notes: Beef and vegetables freeze well. Lettuce must be fresh weekly - or substitute cabbage cups.

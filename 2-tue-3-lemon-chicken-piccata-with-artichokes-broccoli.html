2 Tue 3: Lemon Chicken Piccata with Artichokes & Broccoli

Per serving: ~510 cal, 48g protein, 14g carbs, 28g fat. Tuesday — Liver Support theme. Capers + artichokes + lemon for bile flow.

Servings: 4 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 25 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 jar (14 oz) artichoke hearts in olive oil, drained and halved
  • 1.5 lbs broccoli florets
  • 1/3 cup capers, drained
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in skillet. Sear chicken 5 minutes per side until browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  2. In same pan, saute garlic 30 seconds. Add lemon juice and chicken broth. Simmer 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in artichokes and capers.
  4. In separate pan or oven: roast broccoli with remaining olive oil and salt at 425F for 15 minutes.
  5. Slice chicken. Divide chicken with piccata sauce over broccoli in 4 containers. Cool, freeze.

Notes: Capers add brininess that survives freezing well.

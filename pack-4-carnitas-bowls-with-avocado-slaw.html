Pack 4: Carnitas Bowls with Avocado & Slaw

Per serving: ~530 cal, 48g protein, 15g carbs, 32g fat. Week 3 rotation.

Servings: 5 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 6 hours (slow cook) or 45 minutes (pressure cook)
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs pork shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 orange (juice only)
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 lbs cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, minced
  • 2 limes
  • 5 avocados (store whole, cut day-of)

Directions

  1. Slow cook pork with orange juice, lime, garlic, spices on low 6-8 hours (or pressure cook 45 minutes). Shred.
  2. Crisp shredded pork in skillet with olive oil for 5 minutes until edges are golden.
  3. Toss cabbage with cilantro, jalapeno, lime juice - this is your slaw base.
  4. Portion pork into 5 freezer containers. Pack slaw into 5 fridge containers.
  5. Slice fresh avocado at lunch and add.

Notes: Pork freezes beautifully. Slaw fresh in fridge. Avocado day-of only.

Categories: Lunch, Grain-Free, Week 3

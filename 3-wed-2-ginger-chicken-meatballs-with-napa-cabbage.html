3 Wed 2: Ginger Chicken Meatballs with Napa Cabbage

Per serving: ~520 cal, 50g protein, 12g carbs, 30g fat. Wednesday — Metabolic/Insulin theme. Ginger and 5-spice support insulin sensitivity.

Servings: 4 Prep: 20 minutes Cook: 16 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs ground chicken (thigh preferred for moisture)
  • 3 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos (divided)
  • 1 tsp Chinese 5-spice
  • 2 lbs napa cabbage, thick-sliced into 1-inch ribbons
  • 1 lb shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 tbsp avocado oil
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Mix ground chicken, half the ginger, garlic, 2 tbsp coconut aminos, 5-spice, and salt. Form 16 meatballs (4 per serving).
  2. Heat 2 tbsp avocado oil in skillet. Brown meatballs all over, 8 minutes. Remove.
  3. In same pan, add remaining oil and mushrooms. Cook 3 minutes.
  4. Add napa cabbage, remaining ginger, remaining coconut aminos, and sesame oil. Cook 5 minutes until cabbage wilts but retains some bite.
  5. Return meatballs. Simmer 3 minutes.
  6. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Napa cabbage replaces bok choy (which turns watery and pale when frozen).

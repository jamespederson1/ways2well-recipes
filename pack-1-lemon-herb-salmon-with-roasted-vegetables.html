Pack 1: Lemon Herb Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

Per serving: ~520 cal, 46g protein, 14g carbs, 30g fat. Pack 1 anchor (also used in Pack 3 - Comfort Classics).

Servings: 5 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 35 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 lbs salmon fillets, skin removed, cut into 5 portions
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp dried dill
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 lbs Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 1.5 lbs asparagus, ends trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 lb bell peppers (mixed colors), cut into chunks
  • 4 tbsp olive oil (for vegetables)
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425F. Line two sheet pans with parchment.
  2. Toss Brussels sprouts, asparagus, and bell peppers with olive oil, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Spread across both pans (don't crowd).
  3. Roast 20 minutes, stirring once.
  4. Meanwhile, brush salmon with olive oil, lemon juice, dill, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. After vegetables have roasted 20 minutes, push them aside and add salmon to the pans. Roast another 10-12 minutes until salmon flakes.
  5. Cool, then portion vegetables and salmon into 5 containers.

Notes: Reheat salmon gently: 2 minutes microwave, rest, 1 more minute.

4 Thu 1: Italian Sausage & Pepper Skillet

Per serving: ~540 cal, 44g protein, 14g carbs, 34g fat. Thursday — Strength & Minerals theme. Pork sausage for zinc.

Servings: 4 Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 20 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs sugar-free Italian sausage, sliced (Bilinski's, Applegate, or Aidells)
  • 3 bell peppers (mixed colors), sliced
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 1 lb cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in large skillet. Brown sausage slices, 5 minutes. Remove.
  2. Add onions and peppers. Cook 6 minutes. Add mushrooms, garlic, and Italian seasoning. Cook 4 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, return sausage. Simmer 8 minutes.
  4. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Sausage brand must be sugar-free. Check label — Bilinski's is reliable.

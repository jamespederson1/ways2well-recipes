Pack 1: Moroccan Lamb Tagine

Per serving: ~540 cal, 46g protein, 18g carbs, 30g fat. Week 2 rotation.

Servings: 5 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 35 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 lbs ground lamb (or lean ground beef)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large onions, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp ras el hanout (or 1 tsp each cumin, coriander, cinnamon, paprika, ginger)
  • 1.5 lbs cauliflower florets, cut into 1.5-inch pieces
  • 1 jar (14 oz) artichoke hearts in olive oil, drained and quartered
  • 1 lb cremini mushrooms, quartered
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup pitted green olives
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint and parsley, chopped
  • Salt, pepper, lemon juice

Directions

  1. Brown lamb in olive oil over medium-high heat, 8 minutes. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add onions and garlic, cook 4 minutes. Stir in spices and cook 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add cauliflower, mushrooms, tomatoes, and broth. Simmer covered 15-18 minutes until cauliflower is tender. Stir in artichoke hearts during last 3 minutes (they just need warming through).
  4. Stir in olives and lemon juice. Cool, portion into 5 containers.
  5. Top with fresh herbs after reheating.

Notes: Freezes excellently - flavors actually improve with time.

1 Mon 1: Lemon Herb Salmon with Brussels & Green Beans

Per serving: ~520 cal, 46g protein, 14g carbs, 30g fat. Monday — Lipid & Cardio theme. Freezer-to-plate, no fresh additions required.

Servings: 4 Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 20 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs salmon fillets (skin-off preferred for freezer)
  • 1.5 lbs Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 1.2 lbs fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil (divided)
  • 3 lemons (2 juiced, 1 sliced into rounds)
  • 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Toss Brussels sprouts and green beans with 3 tbsp olive oil, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Spread on sheet pan.
  2. Arrange salmon on top of vegetables. Drizzle with remaining 1 tbsp olive oil and juice of 2 lemons. Top with lemon rounds.
  3. Bake 18-20 minutes until salmon flakes. Cool completely.
  4. Divide into 4 freezer containers. Freeze flat.
  5. To reheat: Thaw overnight in fridge. Oven 350F covered 18-22 min, or microwave 3-4 min with damp paper towel on top.

Notes: Green beans replace asparagus (which gets stringy and mushy when frozen).

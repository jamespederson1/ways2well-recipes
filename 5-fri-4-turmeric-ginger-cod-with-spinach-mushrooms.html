5 Fri 4: Turmeric-Ginger Cod with Spinach & Mushrooms

Per serving: ~490 cal, 48g protein, 12g carbs, 26g fat. Friday — Anti-Inflammatory theme. Turmeric + ginger + black pepper combo.

Servings: 4 Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 18 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs cod fillets (thick center-cut)
  • 2 lbs fresh spinach
  • 1 lb cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp ground turmeric
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp avocado oil
  • 1 tbsp black pepper (enhances turmeric absorption)
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Heat avocado oil in large skillet. Add mushrooms, cook 4 minutes. Add garlic, ginger, turmeric, and black pepper, 1 minute.
  2. Pour in coconut milk, simmer 3 minutes.
  3. Add spinach in batches, wilting down. Season with salt.
  4. Nestle cod fillets into sauce. Cover, simmer 8-10 minutes until cod flakes.
  5. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Black pepper is critical - piperine increases turmeric bioavailability 2000%.

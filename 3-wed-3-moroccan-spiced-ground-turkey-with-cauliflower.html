3 Wed 3: Moroccan-Spiced Ground Turkey with Cauliflower

Per serving: ~500 cal, 48g protein, 14g carbs, 28g fat. Wednesday — Metabolic/Insulin theme. Cinnamon clinically lowers fasting glucose.

Servings: 4 Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 25 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs ground turkey (93/7)
  • 1.5 lbs cauliflower florets
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp ras el hanout
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional — skip for stricter low-glycemic)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in large skillet. Brown turkey, breaking up, 5 minutes.
  2. Add onion and garlic, cook 4 minutes. Add ras el hanout and cinnamon, stir 1 minute.
  3. Add cauliflower and tomatoes. Simmer covered 15 minutes until cauliflower is tender.
  4. Stir in raisins if using. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Omit raisins for stricter glucose control.

4 Thu 3: Beef Stroganoff with Cauliflower Rice

Per serving: ~520 cal, 46g protein, 12g carbs, 32g fat. Thursday — Strength & Minerals theme. Mushrooms add ergothioneine.

Servings: 4 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 20 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs beef sirloin, thin-sliced
  • 1.5 lbs cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1.5 lbs frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tbsp arrowroot starch (or cornstarch)
  • 3 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil, sear beef in batches, 90 seconds per batch. Remove.
  2. In same pan, saute onion and mushrooms 6 minutes. Add garlic and paprika, 1 minute.
  3. Whisk arrowroot into cold beef broth until smooth. Pour into pan, simmer 2 minutes until lightly thickened.
  4. Add heavy cream, dijon, and vinegar. Simmer 2 minutes. Stir in parmesan until melted.
  5. Return beef. Warm through 2 minutes. Do not boil.
  6. Cook cauliflower rice in dry skillet 6-8 minutes until dry. Drain any residual liquid.
  7. Layer cauliflower rice in 4 containers, top with stroganoff. Cool, freeze.

Notes: Cream cheese (which breaks under microwave reheat) replaced with arrowroot-stabilized heavy cream + extra dijon as the emulsifier. Tang restored by white wine vinegar + dijon. Microwave-bulletproof.

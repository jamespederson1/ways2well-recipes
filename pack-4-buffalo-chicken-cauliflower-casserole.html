Pack 4: Buffalo Chicken Cauliflower Casserole

Per serving: ~510 cal, 54g protein, 12g carbs, 27g fat. Week 2 rotation.

Servings: 5 Prep: 20 minutes Cook: 45 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs, diced
  • 2 tbsp avocado oil
  • 2 lbs cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup buffalo hot sauce (Frank's RedHot or similar)
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar
  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 4 stalks celery, diced
  • 2 tsp garlic powder, salt, pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Toss cauliflower with oil, salt, pepper. Roast 20 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, cook chicken in skillet with avocado oil, garlic powder, salt, pepper until cooked through, 8 minutes.
  3. In a saucepan, melt cream cheese with heavy cream and buffalo sauce until smooth.
  4. Combine chicken, roasted cauliflower, celery, and sauce in a baking dish. Top with cheddar and blue cheese. Bake 15 minutes until bubbly.
  5. Cool, portion into 5 containers.

Notes: Casserole-style freezes very well.

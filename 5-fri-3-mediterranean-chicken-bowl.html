5 Fri 3: Mediterranean Chicken Bowl

Per serving: ~500 cal, 46g protein, 16g carbs, 28g fat. Friday — Anti-Inflammatory theme. Heavy EVOO and oregano for polyphenols.

Servings: 4 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 15 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, cubed
  • 1.5 lbs frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts in olive oil, drained
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 can (14 oz) fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 4 tbsp olive oil (divided)
  • 3 tbsp dried oregano
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Toss chicken with 2 tbsp olive oil, oregano, garlic, salt, and pepper. Sear in skillet 8 minutes.
  2. Add canned tomatoes to the chicken pan, simmer 8 minutes until thickened and jammy. Stir in artichokes and olives during last 2 minutes.
  3. Cook cauliflower rice in dry skillet 6-8 minutes until water evaporates. Toss with remaining olive oil, lemon juice, and salt.
  4. Layer cauliflower rice in 4 containers, top with chicken mixture. Cool, freeze.

Notes: Fire-roasted canned tomatoes replace fresh cherry tomatoes (which burst when frozen).

Categories: Lunch, Grain-Free, Friday

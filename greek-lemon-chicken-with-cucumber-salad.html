Greek Lemon Chicken with Cucumber Salad

Per serving: ~500 cal, 50g protein, 14g carbs, 26g fat. Week 3 rotation.

Servings: 5 Prep: 25 minutes Cook: 30 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup olive oil (plus 2 tbsp)
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1.5 lbs bell peppers (mixed colors), cut into chunks
  • 0.5 lb red onion, cut into wedges
  • 3 cucumbers, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 8 oz feta
  • 1/4 cup olive oil (for cucumber salad)
  • Lemon, fresh dill

Directions

  1. Marinate chicken 20 minutes in oil, lemon, garlic, oregano. Grill or pan-sear 7 minutes per side.
  2. Roast peppers and red onion at 425F with olive oil and salt for 25 minutes.
  3. Slice chicken, portion with roasted vegetables into 5 freezer containers.
  4. Make cucumber salad fresh (cucumbers, tomatoes, red onion, feta, olive oil, lemon, dill) and store in 5 small fridge containers.
  5. Assemble at lunch by combining chicken/veg with the cucumber salad.

Notes: Chicken and roasted veg freeze well. Cucumber salad is fresh-only.

Categories: Lunch, Grain-Free, Week 3

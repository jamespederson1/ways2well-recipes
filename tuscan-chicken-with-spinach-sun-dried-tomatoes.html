Tuscan Chicken with Spinach & Sun-Dried Tomatoes

Per serving: ~530 cal, 52g protein, 12g carbs, 30g fat. Week 2 rotation.

Servings: 5 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 25 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained and chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1.5 lbs fresh spinach
  • 2 lbs zucchini noodles (spiralized)

Directions

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in large skillet, sear chicken 6-7 minutes until golden and cooked through. Set aside.
  2. In same pan, saute garlic 1 minute. Add sun-dried tomatoes, cream, broth, parmesan, Italian seasoning. Simmer 3 minutes until thickened.
  3. Add spinach in batches until wilted. Return chicken to pan.
  4. Saute zucchini noodles separately for 2 minutes - DO NOT freeze them with the sauce.
  5. Portion chicken/sauce into 5 containers for freezing. Store raw zucchini noodles separately in fridge or buy fresh weekly.

Notes: Zucchini noodles must be fresh - sauce them day-of (2 minutes).

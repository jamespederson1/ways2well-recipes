2 Tue 1: Moroccan Lamb Tagine

Per serving: ~540 cal, 46g protein, 16g carbs, 30g fat. Tuesday — Liver Support theme. Artichokes and cauliflower for ALT reduction.

Servings: 4 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 25 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs ground lamb (or 90/10 ground beef)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp ras el hanout
  • 1.2 lbs cauliflower florets
  • 1 jar (14 oz) artichoke hearts in olive oil, drained and quartered
  • 1 lb cremini mushrooms, quartered
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Brown lamb in olive oil over medium-high heat, 6 minutes. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add onion and garlic, cook 4 minutes. Stir in ras el hanout, cook 1 minute.
  3. Add cauliflower, mushrooms, and tomatoes. Simmer covered 15 minutes until cauliflower tender.
  4. Stir in artichokes. Cook 3 minutes to warm through.
  5. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Artichokes provide silymarin-like cynarin for liver support.

Pack 1: Ginger Chicken Meatballs with Bok Choy

Per serving: ~510 cal, 52g protein, 12g carbs, 27g fat. Week 3 rotation.

Servings: 5 Prep: 25 minutes Cook: 20 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs ground chicken
  • 2 tbsp grated ginger (plus 1 tbsp for sauce)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 scallions, minced
  • 2 tbsp coconut aminos (plus 1/4 cup for sauce)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (plus 1 tbsp for sauce)
  • 1 egg
  • Salt, pepper
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 3 tbsp avocado oil (for cooking)
  • 2 lbs baby bok choy, halved
  • 1 lb shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar (for sauce)

Directions

  1. Mix ground chicken with ginger, garlic, scallions, coconut aminos, sesame oil, egg, salt, pepper. Form into 25 meatballs.
  2. Brown meatballs in avocado oil over medium heat, 8-10 minutes total. Set aside.
  3. In same pan, saute mushrooms 4 minutes, then bok choy 3 minutes until just wilted.
  4. Whisk sauce ingredients (coconut aminos, rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger), pour over everything in pan, simmer 2 minutes.
  5. Portion 5 meatballs and vegetables per container.

Notes: Excellent freezer recipe - reheats perfectly.

5 Fri 1: Tuscan Chicken with Spinach & Sun-Dried Tomatoes

Per serving: ~530 cal, 50g protein, 14g carbs, 30g fat. Friday — Anti-Inflammatory theme. Spinach + sun-dried tomato lycopene.

Servings: 4 Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 20 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 2.5 lbs fresh spinach (wilts dramatically)
  • 1 lb cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained and chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil. Sear chicken 6 minutes until golden. Set aside.
  2. Saute mushrooms 4 minutes. Add garlic, 30 seconds.
  3. Add sun-dried tomatoes, tomato sauce, and Italian seasoning. Simmer 5 minutes until thickened.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and parmesan. Simmer 2 minutes until parmesan melts and sauce is rosé-colored.
  5. Add spinach in batches, wilting each batch before adding more. 2.5 lbs wilts to ~3 cups.
  6. Return chicken. Warm through.
  7. Cool, portion into 4 containers.

Notes: Switched from a pure heavy-cream sauce (which can break under microwave reheat) to a tomato-cream rosé. Tomato carries the body of the sauce; the smaller cream addition rides along stably. Tuscan flavor profile preserved, microwave-bulletproof.

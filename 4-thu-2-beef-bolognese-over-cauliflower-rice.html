4 Thu 2: Beef Bolognese over Cauliflower Rice

Per serving: ~530 cal, 48g protein, 14g carbs, 30g fat. Thursday — Strength & Minerals theme. Grass-fed beef for iron and zinc.

Servings: 4 Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 25 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs grass-fed ground beef (85/15)
  • 1.5 lbs frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup parmesan, grated (divided)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Brown beef in olive oil, 6 minutes. Drain excess fat. Add onion and garlic, cook 4 minutes. Add Italian seasoning, 1 minute.
  2. Add tomato sauce and diced tomatoes. Simmer 15 minutes until thick. Stir in 1/4 cup parmesan.
  3. Cook cauliflower rice in dry skillet over medium-high 6-8 minutes, stirring often, until water evaporates.
  4. Toss dried cauliflower rice with butter, remaining parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  5. Layer cauliflower rice in 4 containers, top with bolognese. Cool completely, freeze flat.

Notes: Cauliflower rice replaces mashed (which waters out when frozen).

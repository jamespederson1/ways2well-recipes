4 Thu 4: Lamb Shepherd's Pie with Cauliflower Top

Per serving: ~540 cal, 44g protein, 16g carbs, 32g fat. Thursday — Strength & Minerals theme. Lamb is the highest-zinc red meat.

Servings: 4 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 30 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs ground lamb (or 50/50 lamb-beef blend)
  • 2 lbs frozen cauliflower florets
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, small-diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp dried rosemary
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in skillet. Brown lamb 6 minutes. Drain excess fat. Add onion, carrots, and garlic, cook 6 minutes.
  2. Stir in tomato paste, Worcestershire, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper. Cook 2 minutes.
  3. Steam cauliflower 12 minutes until very soft. Drain exhaustively (critical for freezer texture). Mash with butter, cream, and salt.
  4. Layer lamb mixture in 4 oven-safe containers, top with cauliflower mash. Cool completely before freezing.
  5. To reheat: Bake at 375F for 25 minutes from frozen, or 15 minutes from thawed.

Notes: Requires oven reheat — do not microwave this one.

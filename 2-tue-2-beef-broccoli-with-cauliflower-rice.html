2 Tue 2: Beef & Broccoli with Cauliflower Rice

Per serving: ~510 cal, 50g protein, 14g carbs, 28g fat. Tuesday — Liver Support theme. Heavy cruciferous (broccoli + cauliflower).

Servings: 4 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 18 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs flank steak, thin-sliced against the grain
  • 2 lbs broccoli florets
  • 1.5 lbs cauliflower rice (frozen is fine)
  • 1/2 cup coconut aminos
  • 3 tbsp avocado oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat 2 tbsp avocado oil in large skillet over high heat. Sear beef in batches, 2 minutes per batch. Remove.
  2. In same pan, add remaining oil and broccoli. Stir-fry 4 minutes. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute.
  3. Return beef. Add coconut aminos and sesame oil. Toss 2 minutes.
  4. Cook cauliflower rice in separate dry skillet 6-8 minutes until water evaporates.
  5. Layer cauliflower rice on bottom of 4 containers, top with beef and broccoli. Cool, freeze.

Notes: Dry-pan cauliflower rice before portioning to prevent watery texture.

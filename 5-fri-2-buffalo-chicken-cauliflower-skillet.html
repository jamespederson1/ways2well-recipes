5 Fri 2: Buffalo Chicken & Cauliflower Skillet

Per serving: ~520 cal, 48g protein, 12g carbs, 30g fat. Friday — Anti-Inflammatory theme. Capsaicin from hot sauce supports inflammation markers.

Servings: 4 Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 20 minutes
Tip: Click the AnyList extension icon in your Edge toolbar to import this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.8 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1.5 lbs cauliflower florets, cut small
  • 1/2 cup Frank's RedHot (check no added sugar)
  • 4 tbsp butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar
  • 1/4 cup ranch seasoning (dry blend)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium-high. Sear chicken 6 minutes until cooked through and lightly browned. Remove.
  2. In same pan, add cauliflower florets and chicken broth. Cover, steam 5-6 minutes until just tender. Uncover and let any remaining liquid evaporate.
  3. Reduce heat to low. Add melted butter, Frank's, ranch seasoning, and cream cheese. Whisk until cream cheese fully melts into a smooth sauce (2-3 minutes).
  4. Return chicken. Toss to coat. Sprinkle cheddar over the top and stir into the mixture as it melts (do not leave as a separate layer).
  5. Cool, portion into 4 containers, freeze.

Notes: Replaces the original casserole (which needed oven baking to set the cheese top — doesn't work for microwave-only). Cheese is now fully melted into the sauce body, so reheating just warms the dish without needing to bake anything. Same buffalo + ranch + cheddar flavor profile.
